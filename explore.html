#WhyITravel

Stepping outside the comforts of one's own familiar settings is a combination of all that sets us free as a people. It elicits anxiety and excitement, worry and wonder. It keeps us alive and moving forward.

Without travel, I would not be the man I am today. An exploration of this globe has added a great deal of tolerance, understanding, empathy and humility to who I am as a person.

I am better because of travel, I am happier because of travel and I am richer because of travel; not always in my pockets, but in my soul.

Matthew J. Hughes
