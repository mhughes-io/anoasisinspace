Thank You & Farewell

6 August 1984 - Now

Between that dash, I have had the fortune of living an absolutely amazing life. One filled with love, humor, wonder and adventure. My greatest passion in this world is to travel. To witness the beauty of this planet firsthand and immerse myself in a variety of cultures. If there's any comfort to be had in this untimely event, it's in the knowledge that as death approached, I couldn't have felt any more alive. I was continuing to explore, I was being true to myself.

Our time on this earth is much more finite than we may choose to imagine. We are not owed one second beyond the very one which we currently possess. If there were anything I could ask of those reading these words, it'd be as follows: be present, be kind and live with no regrets. If you love somebody, tell them so. If you want to go somewhere, go. If there's something in your life that's making you unhappy, change it. If you want to dance, then freaking dance!

This is not a practice run. Death will become us all and to willfully avoid this realization is offering a great disservice to oneself. For when we are permitted to embrace the inevitable, it is at this moment when we truly allow ourselves to live. But now, my time is up, and I must go.

Find peace in this world, my friends. It's the only one we've got.

I love you all.
