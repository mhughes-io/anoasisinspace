Welcome to the hub of my earthly expeditions!

This site was created in an effort to share my adventures with those who I have known for years, as well as those who I have yet to meet. It offers honest guidance through the lens of a seasoned traveler with a focus on culture and nature, history and geography.

In my blogs, I weave storytelling with humor. In my explorations, I aim to inform and inspire. And in my photos, which are intertwined throughout these many pages, I hope to elicit a sense of awe.

For we live in the most ideal world; one equipped with all of the elements necessary to give us life and which permits us to explore the beauty that is all around us.

A perfect planet. An Oasis in Space.

About Me

My name is Matt Hughes and the core of my life is travel. It has fueled my choice of career, shaped where I've resided and rationalized the inherit risks associated with a constant pursuit of exploration.

In an attempt to pinpoint the precise moment in which travel cemented itself as the top priority, I was transported back to the summer of 2006, when a hardworking, Costa Rican family from San Joaquin de Flores welcomed me into their home. Over the course of a few weeks, my outlook on this planet, and those who reside within it, forever changed.

I have since visited over twenty countries, studied in Spain, returned to Costa Rica as a volunteer, gained dual citizenship from the United Kingdom and, as of this writing, set forth on a roughly six-month trek across the globe; no doubt increasing my country total with each passing week.

My motto in life is to be present, be kind and live with no regrets. Each day, I strive to embody these words.
