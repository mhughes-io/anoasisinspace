#WhyIWrite

There is a famous quote, one attributed to the Moroccan traveler, Ibn Battuta, which states that traveling "leaves you speechless, then turns you into a storyteller," so here I am.

I have been enamored with writing for as far back as I can remember, yet have thus far been unable to discover a true outlet. With this page, I dare to realize my passion for the written word, while also incorporating firsthand imagery.

As I recap accounts of lands unfamiliar and otherwise, I aim to shed a light on cultures oft-overlooked, introduce unique perspectives, extend a platform to the voiceless and, above all, simply remember.

Matthew J. Hughes
